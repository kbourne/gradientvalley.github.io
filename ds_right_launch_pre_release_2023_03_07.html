Gradient Valley Launches First of Its Kind Mobile App Showcasing GPT-Based Artificial Intelligence in Education

Ann Arbor, MI - Gradient Valley announces the pre-release of the DS Right mobile app on the Google Play mobile app store, marking a new milestone in mobile app development. The DS Right app integrates advanced artificial intelligence, including OpenAI's ChatGPT technology, to provide always-on tutoring services and in-depth coverage of the highly technical and challenging Data Science field. Users can pre-register to download the Android version of the app here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dsright.appervasive.ds_right. The iOS version will be released soon as well.

"We are excited to launch the DS Right mobile app, which we believe will revolutionize the way students and professionals learn and keep up with Data Science. The integration of advanced artificial intelligence, including ChatGPT technology, provides an unmatched comprehensive and always-on tutoring and learning experience," said Keith Bourne, CEO and founder of Gradient Valley.

DS Right is a first of its kind mobile app, utilizing the power of the ChatGPT platform, as well as the first app to provide full-fledged AI-based tutoring. AI played a significant role in every stage of the app's development, from content generation to ongoing tutoring support. Gradient Valley used a combination of ChatGPT and proprietary internally developed AI to generate a full range of data science content. A team of moderators provide a final round of checks to ensure accuracy, as well as to rate and improve the quality of the output of our own AI models.

The DS Right mobile app is central to Gradient Valley's effort to deliver Data Science supplemental education to professionals and students. The app utilizes an on-demand AI Tutor, a question/answer format that is AI-generated, and thousands of sections covering every aspect of a well-rounded Data Science education. Additionally, the app provides hundreds of interview question sections that focus on typical questions asked in Data Science interviews, covering every aspect the student would need to know to answer the question properly.

Interested users can register to receive the app download automatically once it is released on Google Play. The app will be free to download, providing access to the extensive intro section. Additional features and content, including the AI Tutor, will be available with the DS Pro membership.

Gradient Valley (http://www.gradientvalley.com) is a machine learning and mobile development company focused on creating innovative AI-based products and solutions. The company's mission is to harness the combination of AI and mobile in ways that bring real improvements to the lives of our mobile users.
