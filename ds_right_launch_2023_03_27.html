Gradient Valley Launches First-of-Its Kind Mobile App Showcasing GPT-Based Artificial Intelligence in Education

Ann Arbor, MI - Gradient Valley announces the availability of the DS Right mobile app on the Apple and Google Play app stores, marking a new milestone in mobile and education technology. The DS Right app is a supplemental learning tool for data science professionals that utilizes advanced artificial intelligence (AI), including OpenAI's ChatGPT 4.0 technology. The app provides always-on AI-based tutoring services and in-depth topic coverage of the highly technical and challenging Data Science field. "We are excited to launch the DS Right mobile app. We believe it will be a game-changer for anyone interested in the data science field. The appâ€™s unique combination of AI-driven tutoring, extensive coverage of key topics and interview question sections will provide users with an unparalleled learning experience," said Keith Bourne, CEO and founder of Gradient Valley.

DS Right is a first-of-its kind mobile app, utilizing the power of the 4.0 version of the ChatGPT platform, as well as being the first app to provide full-fledged AI-based tutoring. AI played a significant role in every stage of the app's development, from content generation to ongoing tutoring support. Gradient Valley combined ChatGPT4 with an internally developed, proprietary AI. The combination of the two AI platforms has resulted in a full range of high-quality data science content that continues to expand and cover every possible aspect of data science. A team of moderators provides a final round of checks to ensure accuracy, as well as to rate and improve the quality of the output of Gradient Valley's AI models. Users of the DS Right mobile app can then use the Personal Lesson Planner feature to identify and focus on the topics that best meet their needs.

The DS Right mobile app is central to Gradient Valley's efforts to deliver data science supplemental education to professionals and students. The app utilizes an on-demand AI tutor, an AI generated question/answer format, and thousands of sections covering every aspect of a well-rounded Data Science education. Additionally, the app provides hundreds of interview question sections that focus on typical questions asked in data science interviews, covering every aspect the student or professional would need to know to answer the question properly. Gradient Valley will be launching online classes, video channels, and other related resources in coordination with the DS Right app launch, all with the focus of doing "Data Science Right."

Gradient Valley (http://www.gradientvalley.com) is a machine learning and mobile development company on a mission to expand access to education through the application of Artificial Intelligence and mobile/web apps. We beleive a combination of AI and mobile will allow us to bring real improvements to the lives of our mobile users. Gradient Valley is developing a suite of education-based apps utilizing AI to its fullest extent, starting with the DS Right mobile app, and aiming to provide an unmatched comprehensive and always-on tutoring and learning experience.
