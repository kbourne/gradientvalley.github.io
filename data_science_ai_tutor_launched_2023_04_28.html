Gradient Valley Launches Fully Integrated Data Science AI Tutor Mobile App For iOS and Android

Ann Arbor, MI - Gradient Valley announces a next level data science tutoring app, with a fully integrated AI tutor that participates in each lesson, providing feedback and information throughout their data science learning process. Gradient Valley has two mobile apps, DS Right, with a focus on general data science learning, and DS Interviews, with a focus on acing the interview process for data scientists. And the new AI Tutoring experience is available to all users of the app, including the free version as part of the always free Unit 1 section of the app. The DS Right and DS Interview apps are supplemental learning tools for data science professionals that utilize advanced artificial intelligence (AI), including OpenAI's ChatGPT 4.0 technology. The apps provide always-on AI-based tutoring services and in-depth topic coverage of the highly technical and challenging Data Science field. Numerous research papers have been published pointing to the efficacy of strong online social presence being tied to improved learning outcomes. In the earliest days of online education[1], this topic was highly researched and continues to be a focus, with an overwhelming amount of support for strong links between online social presence and improved learning outcomes[2][3]. In recent years, significant movement to utilizing both computers and mobile devices as primary learning tools has been accelerated with events like the COVID-19 outbreaks and major disasters like hurricanes that can wipe out entire universities for months or longer [4]. Gradient Valley is utilizing this concept in the mobile app space to help improve how learners gain the knowledge they need in today's competitive data science landscape. The following images show the significant interactivity between the user and the AI Tutor that helps make this a reality:

DS Right and DS Interviews are first-of-their-kind mobile apps, utilizing the power of the 4.0 version of the ChatGPT platform, as well as being the first apps to provide full-fledged AI-based tutoring integrated directly into the main functionality of the app. AI played a significant role in every stage of the app's development, from content generation to ongoing tutoring support. Gradient Valley combined ChatGPT4 with an internally developed, proprietary AI. The combination of the two AI platforms has resulted in a full range of high-quality data science content that continues to expand and cover every possible aspect of data science. A team of moderators provides a final round of checks to ensure accuracy, as well as to rate and improve the quality of the output of Gradient Valley's AI models. Users of the DS Right and DS Interview mobile apps can then use the Personal Lesson Planner feature to identify and focus on the topics that best meet their needs.
